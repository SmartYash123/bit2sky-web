About Bit2Sky SMS Notifications

What messages will I receive?

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Telehealth session links and notifications
  • Account verification codes
  • Prescription and care coordination updates

Sample Messages:

  • Bit2Sky: Your appointment with Dr. Smith is confirmed for March 10 at 2:00 PM. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Bit2Sky: Reminder - You have a telehealth visit tomorrow at 10:00 AM. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
  • Bit2Sky: Your verification code is 123456. This code expires in 10 minutes.

Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You can also manage SMS preferences in your Bit2Sky account settings.

Help: Reply HELP or contact info@bit2sky.com

Supported Carriers: AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and most major US carriers.
